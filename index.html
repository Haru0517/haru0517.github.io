About Me

Yuto Chikazawa

Programmer & Data Analyst from Kyoto

I belonged to ProntiatVR of Doshisha Roam Project. It is an organization where students act actively, neither lectures nor clubs. We created VR game "Yozakura Wizard VR", and are publishing on Steam. We advanced to the final selection at the amateur division of Japanese Game Awards in 2017. And we exhibited in indie type A of Tokyo Game Show 2018. Apart from that, I'm researching on "Author Identification" in academic databases of different languages by data analysis. I have been developing games, but I am also interested in other software engineer, support engineer or data analyst.
